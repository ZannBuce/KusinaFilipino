About Kusina Filipino

Filipino dishes are distinguished by distinct flavors and textures rather than by different courses. Instead of serving courses separately, they are all brought to the table at the same time, allowing diners to enjoy all flavors and dishes at the same time.

Kusina Filipino is about Filipino Food and promoting Filipino Food, Filipino Dish represents on how Filipinos are good at cooking and can par with other countries, Talented Filipino Chefs compete in other countries to prove others that we are capable of having a world class Dish and that other people around the globe should try Filipino Dish, if they tried it is impossible for them to forget how good Filipino Dishes are.

At Kusina Filipino, we encourage you to be adventurous with your food. Kusina Filipino is here to help you no matter what or how you prepare that special dish. You can rely on us to save time in the kitchen with smart cooking hacks and tips. You'll be surprised at how easy it is to make delectable food and desserts from scratch to satisfy your cravings and complement your lifestyle.
